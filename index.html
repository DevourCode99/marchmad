Scoring

The traditional March Madness scoring method awards points for correctly picking winners in each round, typically doubling each round as follows:

  • Round of 64: 1 point per correct pick
  • Round of 32: 2 points per correct pick
  • Sweet 16: 4 points per correct pick
  • Elite 8: 8 points per correct pick
  • Final Four: 16 points per correct pick
  • Championship Game: 32 points for correctly picking the champion

This scoring method places increasing value on correctly picking winners in later rounds.
